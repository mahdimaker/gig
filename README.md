# GIG DRIVER

A modern financial analytics platform for rideshare and delivery drivers.

GIG DRIVER helps gig workers understand their **true earnings**, not just their gross income. By accounting for fuel costs, vehicle depreciation, tolls, parking, and other operating expenses, it provides an accurate picture of real profitability after every shift.

---

## 🚀 Features

### 📊 Smart Shift Tracking

- Record gross earnings
- Track distance traveled
- Log online working hours
- Select one or multiple gig platforms
- Add tolls, parking, car wash, and custom expenses

### 💰 True Net Profit Calculation

Automatically calculates:

- Net Profit
- Hourly Net Earnings
- Profit per Mile / Kilometer
- Fuel Cost Estimates
- Vehicle Depreciation
- Expense Ratios

### 📈 Financial Analytics

Analyze your driving business with detailed statistics:

- Gross Revenue
- True Net Profit
- Profit Margin
- Break-Even Hourly Rate
- Total Distance
- Working Hours
- Cost Breakdown

### 🧠 Strategic Insights

Built-in analytics help identify:

- Low-profit shifts
- Excessive idle time
- Inefficient routes
- Best-performing gig platforms
- Profit optimization opportunities

### 🏆 Platform Comparison

Compare your performance across multiple gig services, including:

- Uber
- Lyft
- DoorDash
- Uber Eats
- Amazon Flex
- Instacart
- And many more

Rank platforms by:

- Net Profit
- Hourly Earnings
- Profit Margin
- Shift Performance

### 🚗 Vehicle Calibration

Personalize calculations using:

- Fuel Type
- Fuel Economy
- Fuel Prices
- Depreciation Rate
- Metric / US Imperial / UK Units

### 📂 Shift History

- Complete shift history
- Search and filters
- Edit and delete records
- Detailed expense breakdown
- Notes for every shift

### 💾 Data Management

- CSV Export
- CSV Import
- Backup & Restore

## 🌍 Built for Drivers Worldwide

GIG DRIVER supports multiple measurement systems:

- Metric (km / L)
- US Imperial (mi / gal)
- UK Hybrid (mi / L)

The application automatically adapts to regional preferences while allowing manual switching at any time.
